What is it?

StickIt is a modernized and enhanced version of the original Headhesive.js by Mark Goodyear, thoughtfully updated and maintained by Chayson Media Group. This streamlined module allows you to transform any header into a sticky element that "locks" into place at a precise point on your page. Whether you choose a numeric offset or reference an existing element, StickIt ensures a seamless, user-friendly experience that enhances navigation and overall site usability.
